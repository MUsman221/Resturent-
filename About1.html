About Old Town Restaurant

Welcome to Old Town Restaurant! where art of culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. We take pride in offering a diverse culinary experience that ranges from sizzling BBQ to delightful breakfast, mouthwatering desserts, refreshing drinks, and authentic Middle Eastern cuisine.
Our journey began with a passion for bringing people together through unforgettable flavors. Our talented chefs, each with their own expertise, pour their creativity and dedication into every dish we serve. Whether you're craving bold smokiness of our BBQ, the comforting goodness of our breakfast classics, the sweetness of our desserts, or rich traditions of Middle Eastern cuisine, we have something to delight every palate.
